ABOUT THE TRAINER

From the Pitch to
Your Development

I'm Danny — a current Division I collegiate soccer player born and raised in Brookline, MA. I played at the club and high school levels, including on Intercontinental Football Academy and a championship-winning Brookline High School team. I now play at Temple University (NCAA D1) and in the USL2 league with Seacoast United.

Each session is catered to what you want to improve — whether that's technical fundamentals, tactical awareness, or physical conditioning — and I work with players of all ages and skill levels. Every session, you'll leave a better player than when you walked in. Don't believe me? Your first session is free — come out, put in the work, and feel the difference yourself.
